About Site Noctum

Site Noctum is one of the Foundation's most advanced and enigmatic facilities, veiled in secrecy and cutting-edge technology. This site specializes in the containment and research of high-risk SCP entities, focusing on the obscure and the unknown.

Equipped with advanced containment measures and a staff of elite personnel, Site Noctum stands at the forefront of the Foundation's efforts to protect humanity.
